Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles as per package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce with onion, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Let rest before serving.
