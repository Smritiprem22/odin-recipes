Mango Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1.5 fluid ounces rum cream liqueur (such as RumChataÂ®)
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces mango mixer
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces vanilla vodka
  • 2 fluid ounces chilled ginger ale
  • ice, as needed

Steps

  1. Scoop ice into a shaker.
  2. Pour in rum liqueur, mango mixer, and vanilla vodka.
  3. Cover shaker, and shake until the outside of shaker is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  4. Add ginger ale to shaker and swirl lightly.
  5. Pour drink into a glass. Serve.
